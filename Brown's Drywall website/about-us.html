Our History

Founded in 2021 by Kyle Brown, Brown’s Top Tier Drywall began as a small family business with a big dream — to offer dependable, high-quality drywall services to the Lethbridge community. Over the years, our dedication to craftsmanship and honesty has helped us grow into one of Lethbridge’s most trusted drywall contractors, built on reputation, hard work, and family values.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to deliver top-quality drywall services with a commitment to customer satisfaction, reliability, and professionalism. We take pride in every job we do, ensuring each project is completed with the same care and precision we would expect in our own home.

Meet the Team

Brown’s Top Tier Drywall is a proud family-owned business led by Kyle Brown, who works hands-on with every project to ensure quality and professionalism. Alongside him is his brother Allan, whose craftsmanship and attention to detail help bring each project to life. The team is rounded out by Cheyenne, Kyle’s wife, who manages client relations and social media — keeping communication clear and customers informed every step of the way
